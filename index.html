My Work


Packaging

The Arcane Flask packaging is all about making a bold statement with a touch of mystery. The vibrant yellow and purple color scheme catches your eye instantly, symbolizing energy and intrigue. The compact box puts the spotlight on the Arcane typeface, with its unique 30:70 proportions that feel sturdy yet elegant—just like the flask inside. The logo stands front and center, with simple, clean text that doesn't distract from the design. Open the box, and you'll find the flask cradled in a sleek black insert, making the unboxing feel just as special as the product itself. It's packaging that feels bold, refined, and undeniably memorable—perfect for a brand like Arcane.



Recipe book

Clove Codex is a visually engaging and culturally inspired cookbook that celebrates the versatility of garlic through a Mediterranean lens. The concept revolves around showcasing garlic in its various forms: raw, roasted, pickled, confit, and burnt, each highlighted through innovative recipes. The design integrates bold, playful typography with elegant serif fonts, creating a balance between modernity and tradition. Vibrant Mediterranean patterns, wave inspired motifs, and cozy illustrations bring the pages to life, while dynamic layouts keep the content fresh and immersive. The cookbook's goal is to appeal to a younger audience, blending culinary creativity with a rich visual narrative, making it as much a feast for the eyes as it is for the palate.



Poster Series

This poster series, created as part of a design history project, reimagines three influential art movements: Neoclassicism, Rococo, and Post-Impressionism, through modern product design. Each poster is tied to a product (CD cover, postage stamp, and pizza box) and encapsulates the aesthetic and cultural essence of its respective movement. The Neoclassical poster reflects symmetry and grandeur, the Rococo design emphasizes opulence and whimsy, and the Post-Impressionist piece highlights bold expression and texture. This series bridges historical art traditions with contemporary creativity, showcasing their timeless relevance in modern visual storytelling.
